product

## Users
- **Super Admin (Platform Owner)**: Oversees the SaaS platform, monitors hospital growth/usage analytics, manages subscription plans, and approves onboarding requests.
- **Hospital Admin**: Manages their specific hospital's configuration, department staff, schedules, subscription tier, and reviews revenue/performance analytics.
- **Doctor**: Operates in a high-tempo clinical environment, managing their daily patient queue and token status.
- **Receptionist / Front Desk**: Manages rapid registration, issues tokens for patient appointments, and generates bills and receipts under time pressure.
- **Patient**: Accesses appointment booking and checks live token/queue status online.

## Product Purpose
A multi-tenant Hospital Management System (SaaS) that provides complete data isolation for individual hospitals to manage their scheduling, token queue systems, and billing, with central management for platform owners. Success is measured by rapid data entry at the reception, real-time queue visibility for doctors/patients, and reliable billing.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone**: Calm, professional, and reliable.
- **3-Word Personality**: Efficient, Trustworthy, Precise.
- **Emotional Goals**: Evoke confidence in the hospital staff and calm under high-pressure, fast-paced environments.

## Anti-references
- Avoid cluttered, legacy medical software with unreadably small font sizes, crowded input fields, and heavy borders.
- Avoid overly trendy SaaS landing templates with decorative purple-to-blue gradients, low-contrast text, or nested card layouts.

## Design Principles
1. **Keyboard-First, Mouse-Optional**: Optimize reception and checkout screens to be fully operable via keyboard shortcuts for lightning-fast workflows.
2. **Clear Information Hierarchy**: Present dense data (such as queue metrics or billing line items) using structured grids, clean typography, and purposeful alignment rather than generic card wrapping.
3. **High-Contrast Clarity**: Ensure strong color separation and text-to-background contrast ratios to remain legible in brightly lit reception areas or on low-quality monitors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA color contrast (>= 4.5:1 for normal body text).
- Visible focus outlines and screen-reader accessible form labels.
- Native, robust keyboard navigation flow for all receptionist and doctor-facing actions.
