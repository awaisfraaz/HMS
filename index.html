How Hospital Association & Staff Registration Works

To maintain strict security and patient privacy, Aegis HMS operates on an **invitation-only SaaS model**. Doctors, receptionists, and medical staff cannot register independently. Instead, registration follows a structured chain:

  • 1

    Hospital Onboarding

    A hospital registers as a tenant. This generates a root Hospital Admin account associated with that specific organization.

  • 2

    Platform Approval

    The SaaS platform owner (Super Admin) reviews, authorizes, and activates the hospital's tenant subscription profile.

  • 3

    Staff Onboarding

    The activated Hospital Admin registers doctors, receptionists, and staff profiles inside their dashboard, auto-generating secure logins mapped to their hospital.
